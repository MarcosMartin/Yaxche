Land Banking in Costa Rica

Why Costa Rica, why now

Land banking is the practice of acquiring land for the resources it holds and sustains. It has long been used by well-informed families to build wealth across generations.

Costa Rica combines water security, agricultural productivity, and legal stability in a way that is genuinely uncommon. That combination has gone largely unnoticed by international capital. Yaxche finds, verifies, and presents these opportunities to qualified buyers, and manages the full process from introduction through to closing.

∿

Water Security

Among the most water-rich countries in the hemisphere, with protected resources and strong long-term agricultural potential.

Legal Stability

Strong property rights, a functioning legal system, and decades of political continuity. Not common in this region.

Overlooked Capital
