Why Costa Rica

While Others Look Elsewhere

Those, who think in generations, are quietly securing something no market can manufacture: land with renewable freshwater, political stability, and the ability to produce food.

From family offices in London to sovereign funds in the Gulf, from the mountains of New Zealand to the valleys of Uruguay, the thesis is the same. Land with the right fundamentals holds value across generations, currencies, and cycles.

Costa Rica has every condition this strategy requires. It remains, for now, largely undiscovered.

∿

Freshwater Security

Families who own land with year-round freshwater access in a stable jurisdiction will hold one of the most valuable assets of the next generation. Costa Rica's rivers and aquifers renew continuously. Water is now a traded financial instrument (CME Group, NQH2O).

⚖

Legal and Political Stability

Continuous democracy since 1949. Military abolished constitutionally. Full property rights for foreign nationals, identical to citizens. No approval process. No historical precedent for expropriation. Foreign-owned private land title has never been subject to government seizure. Ranked 58th globally on the 2024 Global Peace Index.

◈

Agricultural Productivity

Volcanic soils and year-round growing seasons support specialty coffee, cacao, tropical fruits, and timber. No off-season. The land produces continuously.

△

Climate Resilience

Elevation buffers against sea-level rise, extreme heat, and hurricane risk. Stable year-round temperate climate.

Investor Residency pathway: A qualifying real estate investment of USD $150,000 makes a foreign national eligible to apply for Costa Rican Investor Residency, including spouse and dependent children. No full-time presence required to maintain status.
How We Work

From Introduction to Legacy

We walk the full path with you. From the moment we present a property through to the day it carries your family's name, and well beyond.

Purchase from anywhere in the world. Build without being there. Hold it, grow it, and pass it on, with a team that manages every detail to the same standard we would apply if it were our own.

1

The Right Opportunity

Every property we present is verified, off-market, and independently assessed. You see only what exceeds the standard.

2

Structure Built for Generations

Our Costa Rican legal counsel reviews title, ownership structure, and advises on the acquisition vehicle best suited to your jurisdiction, your heirs, and your long-term intent. The transaction can be completed from anywhere in the world: Switzerland, Dubai, London, or wherever you are.

3

Tax and Residency, Handled

We coordinate the full tax picture across jurisdictions. For those pursuing Costa Rican Investor Residency, immigration counsel runs in parallel so nothing falls between the gaps.

4

Closing and Beyond

When the transaction closes, the work does not stop. Whether you are building, holding for investment, or preparing a place for your family, we source and advise on construction, design, and operations, drawing only from the top tier of local expertise. Your investment is managed, maintained, and protected for as long as you choose to hold it. Yaxche is compensated through the transaction. There is no advisory fee to the buyer.
